--- title: Welcome ---

I'm a philosopher based at the University of Sydney in the Discipline of Philosophy in the School of Humanities.

I work on a wide range of philosophical topics, but specialise in the philosophy of mind, epistemology, the philosophy of language, and political philosophy.

In the philosophy of mind and epistemology, I have written on such topics as how we know our own reasons for acting and how we know our own moral incapacities. In the philosophy of language, I have written on topics related to the syntax and semantics of why-interrogatives. In political philosophy, I have written on the topic of the relationship between democracy and social equality.
