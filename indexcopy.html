--- title: Welcome ---

I'm a philosopher based at the University of Sydney in the Discipline of Philosophy in the School of Humanities.

I am interested in a wide range of philosophical topics, but specialise in political philosophy, the philosophy of mind and action, and philosophy of language.

I have written on issues relating to our knowledge of our own minds, on issues relating to the syntax and semantics of why-interrogatives, and, more recently, on the relationship between democracy and social equality.

I am currently working on philosophical issues relating to educational justice, political legitimacy, first-person thought, agency, reason for action, and the role of perspectival thought in the humanities.

I live in Sydney with my partner and our three children.

You can find me on PhilPapers here and on PhilPeople here.
